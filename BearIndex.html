About brown bears

The Brown Bear (Ursus Arctos) is native to part of northen Eurasia and North America. its conservation status is currently least consern.

There are many spacies within the brown bear species, includis the Atlas bear and the Himalayan Brown Bear.

Featurs

Brown Bears are not always completely brown.Some can be raddish or yellowish, they have very large, curves claws and huge paws. Male brown bears are often thirty percent larger than female brown bears. They range from 5ft to 9ft from head to toe.
